   - Active vs passive vocabulary

2. **Practical methods**
   - Reading strategies
   - Contextual learning
   - Word roots and etymology

3. **Tools and resources**
   - Apps and software
   - Books and materials

4. **Habit formation**
   - Daily routines
   - Progress tracking
   - Motivation strategies

## Key Insights

- Vocabulary acquisition is most effective when words are learned in context
- Spaced repetition significantly improves retention
- Active use (speaking/writing) reinforces learning better than passive exposure
- Etymology and word families help create mental connections
- Consistency matters more than intensity
