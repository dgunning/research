## Strategic Recommendation

### Primary Recommendation by Scenario

**If we are/have**:

  - Leverage existing M365 licenses
  - Exclusive OpenAI GPT access
  - Strongest compliance (critical for regulated industries)

- **Heavy AWS investment** → **AWS Bedrock**
  - **30-556% documented cost savings**
  - Multi-vendor flexibility avoids lock-in
  - Largest ecosystem, most mature

- **Data analytics focus** → **Google Vertex AI**
  - Best-in-class AutoML
  - 2.8X faster model training (TPUs)
  - Most competitive pricing for capabilities

### If Undecided on Cloud Platform

**Recommend**: AWS Bedrock
- **Why**: Multi-vendor approach provides maximum flexibility
- **Cost**: 10.5% lower 3-year TCO than Azure
- **Risk**: Lowest vendor lock-in exposure

## Critical Success Factors

### Four Non-Negotiables for 2025-2026

1. **Governance Framework** (60% of enterprises will require by 2026)
   - Establish AI governance group with C-level sponsor
   - Implement ISO/IEC 42001 or NIST AI RMF
   - Budget: 5.4% of AI spend ($540K for $10M budget)

2. **Vendor Lock-in Mitigation**
   - Multi-model orchestration architecture
   - Contract clauses: data portability, exit assistance, no egress fees
   - Platform flexibility (especially important given market volatility)

3. **Prepare for AI Agents** (Gartner: 40% of apps by end 2026)
   - Current: <5% of enterprise apps have AI agents
   - 2026: 40% will have them
   - 2035: Potential $450B market (30% of enterprise software revenue)

4. **Organizational Readiness**
   - 20% of organizations will flatten structure by 2026
   - Eliminate 50%+ of middle management positions
   - Prepare workforce for AI augmentation

## Key Risks

### If We Delay Past Q1 2025

❌ **Competitive Disadvantage**: Competitors are deploying now
- 80% of enterprises have 50+ AI use cases in pipeline
- Average 6-18 months to production
- First movers gaining market advantage

❌ **Capacity Constraints**: All major vendors facing supply limitations
- Harder to secure capacity later
- Premium pricing for late entrants

❌ **Talent Gap**: Best AI talent goes to early adopters
- Market for AI expertise increasingly competitive
- Internal expertise takes 12-18 months to develop

### If We Choose Wrong Platform

⚠️ **Lock-in Costs**: 3-5 year commitment with switching costs of 20-40% of total investment
⚠️ **Compliance Gaps**: Regulatory requirements accelerating (60% need formal governance by 2026)
⚠️ **Technical Debt**: Wrong architecture decisions compound over time

### Mitigation

- **Platform Decision**: Q1 2025 using decision framework (see full brief)
- **Governance**: Establish now, required within 12 months anyway
- **Architecture**: Build portability layer from day one (multi-model orchestration)
- **Contracts**: Demand exit clauses and data portability guarantees
