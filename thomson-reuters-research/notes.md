### Research Steps

#### CoCounsel Research (2025-11-20)

**Searches conducted:**
1. "Thomson Reuters CoCounsel AI legal assistant 2025"
2. "CoCounsel AI features capabilities pricing integrations"
3. "CoCounsel Core vs CoCounsel Legal difference features"
4. "Thomson Reuters Casetext acquisition CoCounsel history GPT-4"
5. "Thomson Reuters CoCounsel competitors LexisNexis Harvey AI"

**Key Findings:**

- **Acquisition**: Thomson Reuters acquired Casetext for $650M in August 2023
- **Technology**: Built on OpenAI's GPT-4, launched March 1, 2023
- **Major Launch**: CoCounsel Legal launched August 2025 with agentic AI and Deep Research
- **Adoption**: 20,000+ law firms and corporate legal departments, majority of Am Law 100
- **Pricing**: CoCounsel Core starts at $225/user/month, All Access at $400/month
- **Products**: CoCounsel Essentials (formerly Core) vs CoCounsel Legal (comprehensive)
- **Competitors**: Harvey AI (scored higher in benchmarking), LexisNexis Protégé
- **Integrations**: Microsoft 365, Clio, iManage, NetDocuments, SharePoint, OneDrive

#### Westlaw Research (2025-11-20)

**Searches conducted:**
1. "Westlaw Thomson Reuters legal research platform features"
2. "Westlaw Edge vs Westlaw Precision difference features comparison"
3. "Westlaw history West Publishing Key Number System"

**Key Findings:**

- **History**: Founded by John B. West over 100 years ago, Westlaw launched 1975, acquired by Thomson 1996
- **Key Number System**: Developed 1897-1906, indexes 110,000+ legal topics
- **Content**: 40,000+ databases across 60+ countries
- **Versions**: Westlaw Edge (2018) → Westlaw Precision (2022) → Westlaw Advantage (2025)
- **KeyCite**: Citation checking service to determine if cases/statutes are still good law
- **Integration**: Deep Research now integrated from CoCounsel Legal
- **Precision Features**: 250 attorney editors hand-coded case attributes, claims 50% time savings

- Multi-model approach: OpenAI (primary), Claude/Anthropic (tax/compliance), Google Gemini
- Claude deployed via Amazon Bedrock for security
- RAG architecture connecting to 150+ years of Thomson Reuters content
- Uses Claude 3 Haiku for rapid tasks, Claude 3.5 Sonnet for deep analysis
- Prompt caching for efficiency

**Workflow Examples:**

- Deposition analysis and transcript reviews
- Compliance risk assessments

- Tax memo drafting and jurisdictional comparisons
