## Print & Use Templates

**Recommendation**:
- Print weekly review templates and complete by hand
- Create digital spreadsheet for numerical tracking
- Keep all recordings organized by week/day
- Review progress monthly
- Celebrate improvements regularly

**Progress Photos**:
Consider creating visual progress charts:
- Filler word reduction graph
- Skill rating spider chart
- Speaking pace over time line graph
- Weekly practice completion chart

Track everything. Celebrate progress. Stay consistent.
