## Daily Practice Protocol

### Morning Warm-up (5 minutes)
1. Select 3 words from yesterday's learning
2. Use each in a different register
3. Record yourself for review

### Afternoon Application (10 minutes)
1. Review morning's email/messages
2. Identify 5 vocabulary upgrade opportunities
3. Rewrite with enhancements
4. Note which feel natural vs. forced

### Evening Reflection (5 minutes)
1. Journal about today's vocabulary victories
2. Note any moments of hesitation
3. Plan tomorrow's focus area

## Integration Checklist

Before any presentation or important document:

- [ ] Identified 10 potential vocabulary enhancement points
- [ ] Verified register consistency throughout
- [ ] Checked connotations align with intended tone
- [ ] Ensured no more than 15% enhanced
- [ ] Practiced pronunciation of any challenging words
- [ ] Prepared simpler alternatives if clarification needed
- [ ] Confirmed cultural appropriateness
- [ ] Tested at least 3 enhanced sections aloud
