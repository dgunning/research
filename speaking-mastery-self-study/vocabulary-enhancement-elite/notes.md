## Key Innovations

### The 10-15% Rule
Discovered through research that enhancing more than 15% of words in any communication makes it feel forced and unnatural. Built this limit into all exercises.

### Precision Mapping
Created system for mapping gradients of meaning within concept families, helping users understand subtle distinctions between related words.

### Register Flexibility Training
Developed exercises for switching between five levels of formality, crucial for professional versatility.

### Pressure Performance Focus
Included specific exercises for deploying sophisticated vocabulary under time pressure and stress.

### Native Speaker Advantages
Leveraged crystallized intelligence peaks in 60s-70s and superior context processing abilities of mature speakers.
