# Persuasive Power Words

## Emotional Triggers

### Trust & Credibility Builders
- **Authentic** - genuine and true
- **Proven** - demonstrated by evidence
- **Verified** - confirmed as true
- **Guaranteed** - assured with certainty
- **Certified** - officially recognized
- **Endorsed** - publicly supported
- **Established** - recognized and accepted
- **Trusted** - relied upon with confidence
- **Legitimate** - conforming to law or rules
- **Authoritative** - commanding and confident

### Urgency Creators
- **Immediate** - occurring without delay
- **Instant** - happening at once
- **Imperative** - of vital importance
- **Critical** - at point of crisis
- **Pivotal** - of crucial importance
- **Fleeting** - lasting very short time
- **Ephemeral** - lasting very short time
- **Finite** - having limits
- **Exclusive** - restricted to select few
- **Deadline** - time limit

### Exclusivity & Scarcity
- **Elite** - select group superior in quality
- **Premium** - of exceptional quality
- **Bespoke** - custom-made
- **Curated** - carefully chosen and presented
- **Selective** - carefully chosen
- **Coveted** - greatly desired
- **Rare** - not occurring very often
- **Limited** - restricted in number
- **Distinctive** - characteristic of one thing
- **Privileged** - having special rights

2. Exclusivity + Value: "**Elite** members receive **unprecedented** benefits"

## Caution Notes

### Avoid Overuse
- Deploy strategically, not constantly
- Balance power words with conversational language
- Match intensity to context
- Ensure claims can be substantiated

### Context Appropriateness
- **High Impact Situations**: Use liberally
- **Professional Settings**: Use moderately
- **Academic Context**: Use sparingly
- **Casual Conversation**: Minimize use
