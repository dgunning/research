# SecFlow Design Agency - Business Model & Positioning

## Agency Overview

**Name:** SecFlow Design (working name)

**Tagline:** "Intelligent SEC Filing Workflows - Powered by edgartools"

**Mission:** Transform how organizations access, analyze, and act on SEC filing data through expertly designed AI-augmented workflows.

## Unique Value Proposition

SecFlow Design is the only workflow design agency that combines:
1. **Deep edgartools expertise** - Direct collaboration with edgartools maintainer
2. **AI-native approach** - AI agents work alongside human designers
3. **Production-ready solutions** - Battle-tested workflows, not just prototypes
4. **Hybrid methodology** - Both AI and non-AI workflows for maximum flexibility

## Core Service Offerings

### 1. Workflow Discovery & Design
**What:** Collaborative sessions to understand client needs and design custom SEC data workflows
**Duration:** 2-4 weeks
**Deliverables:**
- Current state analysis
- Workflow architecture diagrams
- Technical specifications
- Implementation roadmap
- ROI projections

**Pricing Model:** Fixed project fee ($25K-$75K depending on complexity)

### 2. Workflow Implementation
**What:** End-to-end development and deployment of SEC filing workflows
**Duration:** 4-12 weeks
**Deliverables:**
- Production-ready code (Python, using edgartools)
- AI agent configurations (if applicable)
- Testing & QA documentation
- Deployment scripts & infrastructure setup
- User documentation

**Pricing Model:**
- Time & materials ($200-$350/hour depending on seniority)
- Or fixed-price ($50K-$250K for complete implementations)

### 3. AI Agent Design & Orchestration
**What:** Design and implementation of multi-agent systems for SEC filing analysis
**Duration:** 6-16 weeks
**Deliverables:**
- Agent architecture design
- Custom agent implementations
- Orchestration layer (LangGraph/LangChain)
- Compliance & audit logging
- Integration with existing systems

**Pricing Model:** Fixed project + ongoing maintenance ($100K-$500K + $5K-$25K/month)

### 4. Workflow Optimization & Consulting
**What:** Retainer-based advisory and optimization services
**Duration:** Ongoing
**Deliverables:**
- Monthly strategy sessions
- Performance optimization
- New feature recommendations
- edgartools upgrade support
- Best practice guidance

**Pricing Model:** Monthly retainer ($10K-$50K/month)

### 5. Training & Enablement
**What:** Team training on edgartools and workflow design best practices
**Duration:** 1-3 days
**Deliverables:**
- Custom training curriculum
- Hands-on workshops
- Code examples & templates
- Reference materials
- Post-training support (30 days)

**Pricing Model:** Per-session fee ($15K-$35K per training engagement)

## Risk Mitigation

### Key Risks & Mitigation Strategies

1. **Risk:** Dependency on single open-source library (edgartools)
   - **Mitigation:** Diversify to adjacent domains, contribute to library development, maintain expertise across multiple tools

2. **Risk:** Regulatory changes affecting SEC data access
   - **Mitigation:** Stay close to regulatory developments, design adaptable workflows, maintain compliance expertise

3. **Risk:** AI technology rapid evolution
   - **Mitigation:** Modular architecture, regular technology reviews, continuous learning culture

4. **Risk:** Client concentration
   - **Mitigation:** Diversify across segments, develop recurring revenue, build product offerings

5. **Risk:** Talent acquisition/retention
   - **Mitigation:** Competitive compensation, equity participation, strong culture, remote-first
